# USTC/THU-SYSU Institutional

- Style ID: `ustc-thu-sysu-institutional`
- Group: `beamer-candidates`
- Demo PPTX: `outputs/style-showcase/beamer-candidates/ustc-thu-sysu-institutional-showcase.pptx`
- Aspect ratio: `16:9`
- Reference: USTC and THU Beamer themes

## Use

Formal university reports, defenses, and institution-facing academic presentations.

## PPTX Spacing Rules

- Use one color variant per full slide in selection decks.
- Use slide titles around 18-30 pt and body text normally at 16 pt or larger.
- Use large visual regions; avoid miniature slide previews.
- Keep 16:9 widescreen dimensions.
